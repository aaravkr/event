— About Event
Motivation & Relevance of the Conference:

The cyberspace is becoming more and more mainstream communication highway and critical information infrastructures are rapidly plugged-in, which needs continuous innovation and research to keep the space secure and resilient to threats. Further, indigenous solutions, in-house expertise and startup/incubation eco-system needs to be created to reduce dependence on foreign products and solutions for securing our critical infrastructure and defense installations.

The workshop will focus on the current trends, need and future requirements of innovation in the field of cybersecurity for a secured cyber space. The workshop will bring speakers from all the stakeholders like Industry, Academia, Government and Non for Profit organizations working in this pace to showcase the innovations, current challenges and future aspects which need to be addressed by the stakeholders.

The digital space is becoming mainstream communication platform for economy and diversified stakeholders are getting benefited with service availability and more transparent process. It’s cutting across geographic barriers and now borderline communities are joining the urban revolution across the nation. The digital space has no boundaries and it works on global standards which are used across the world. The workshop will bring together the innovative research on cyber security from deep pockets across the country and enlighten as well as put focus on the next level of innovation in cyber security domain the country needs to safeguard against cyber threats.

The workshop will help in understanding where we stand, the challenges and what is the way forward for our country in securing our cyber space through homegrown technological research and innovation.


The focus areas:

  1. Innovations in Cyber Security
    Key focus areas from Industry applicable to national security and cyber security posture
  2. Challenges in research model for innovation
    The way ahead - Case Studies for other countries
  3. Critical Internet Infrastructure
    The challenges and scope of innovation
  4. New technologies
    Blockchain, AI, IoT, and their role in securing cyberspace.
  5. The way forward
    more focus on community driven engineering research backed by Industry, Government and other entities leading to more products and standards from our country.
